What You'll Learn

This course focuses on the role of technology in modern business operations. Students will develop proficiency in essential business software applications, including spreadsheets, presentation tools, and communication platforms. Through practical projects, students will learn how to use technology effectively to analyze data, communicate ideas, and solve business problems.
