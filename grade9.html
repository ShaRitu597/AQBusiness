What You'll Learn

This foundational course introduces students to the world of business. Students will explore basic business concepts, learn about different types of businesses, and develop an understanding of how businesses operate in today's economy. Through hands-on projects and real-world examples, students will build essential skills that will serve as a foundation for future business courses.
