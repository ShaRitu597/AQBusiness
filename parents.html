Parent Information

Supporting your child's business education journey

Frequently Asked Questions

How can parents access student progress?

Parents can access student progress through weekly updates via Google Classroom. You will receive invitation links to join as a guardian, allowing you to monitor assignments, grades, and teacher announcements. Regular progress reports are also sent home throughout the semester.

What skills will my child gain?

Students in our Business Studies program develop a comprehensive set of valuable skills, including:

  • Financial literacy and money management
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Leadership and decision-making
  • Digital and technology skills
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving
  • Communication and presentation abilities

Are there extracurricular opportunities?

Yes! Students can participate in several extracurricular activities related to business studies:

  • DECA: An international organization preparing students for careers in marketing, finance, hospitality, and management
  • Entrepreneurship Clubs: Develop and pitch business ideas in a supportive environment
  • Business Competitions: Compete in regional and provincial business case competitions
  • Guest Speaker Series: Learn from local business leaders and entrepreneurs

How can I support my child at home?

Here are some ways you can support your child's business education:

  • Discuss current business news and events
  • Encourage them to manage a small budget or savings goal
  • Support their participation in school business events and competitions
  • Help them explore career options in business fields
  • Attend parent information nights and school events

What are the post-secondary pathways?

Our Business Studies program prepares students for various post-secondary options, including university business programs, college business diplomas, apprenticeships in business-related trades, and direct entry into the workforce with strong foundational skills.
