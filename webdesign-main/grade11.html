Grade 11 offers specialized courses that allow students to explore specific areas of business in depth. Students can choose from Accounting, Marketing, and Entrepreneurship courses, each designed to provide practical skills and theoretical knowledge in these critical business disciplines. These courses prepare students for advanced studies in Grade 12 and beyond.
